About Me

Hello! I am a Research Engineer at GE Vernova specializing in Sustainable Energy Systems based in Niskayuna, New York. Having recently relocated my family from China to the United States to start this new chapter, my core engineering philosophy centers on bridging the gap between cutting-edge theoretical simulation and practical, stable physical deployments.

Prior to my current role in the US, I spent nearly 28 years in China with Shanghai Electric Group Co., Ltd., where I progressed from a foundational systems engineer to Senior Engineer, Project Manager, and Technical Lead. Bringing decades of seasoned international experience, I specialize in transient stability modeling, green energy asset management, complex system reliability optimization, and executing multi-million-dollar energy infrastructure initiatives aimed at meeting aggressive carbon reduction goals.
