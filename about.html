About Me

Hello! I am an Electrical and Power Systems Engineer with over two decades of dedicated experience in grid modernization, sustainable technology implementation, and industrial-scale engineering. Originally from Shanghai, I have recently immigrated to the United States to embark on an exciting new professional chapter as a Research Engineer at GE Vernova's Niskayuna plant in New York.

My core engineering philosophy centers around bridging the gap between cutting-edge theoretical simulation and practical, stable physical deployments. Over my career, I have specialized in the evolution of transient stability modeling, green energy asset management, and complex system reliability optimization.

Driven by a lifelong passion for learning, I continuously evolve my technical stack to meet the demands of modern decarbonization and smart-grid initiatives.
